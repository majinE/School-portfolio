Project 1

Reflection

This first project was quite interesting as I had never really questioned why I was who I am. After being introduced to the term “Discourse Community” I was able to make connections between my history line and how the way a Family Discourse will always has an impact on a person’s personality in all instances. For example, my mother’s family has always been religious; her parenting skills were passed down through generations of religious morals and beliefs. Although I have pushed myself as far as possible form religion, I strongly believe many of my personal morals derive from her parenting skills. I can relate to Jimmy Baca’s experience with his Primary Discourse, as hard as he tried to push himself away from it, the discourse always has some sort of impact on his life. This project also allowed me to search slightly deeper into what my family history.
